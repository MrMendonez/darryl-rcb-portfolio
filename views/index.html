Front-End Web Developer

A high school teacher in South Brunswick, NJ where I teach HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and jQuery in my Web Design classes as well as cloud-based computing and photoshop in my computer classes.

I'm finishing my training to become a full-stack web developer at Rutgers Coding Bootcamp, a rigorous program where I've learned much more about web development including Materialize, APIs, NodeJS, MYSQL, Express, and so much more.

When I'm not working, I feed my love for traveling. I've snowboarded through the evergreen mountains of Vermont and canoed the Bioluminescent Bay of Puerto Rico. I've backpacked from Costa Rica to Nicaragua and hiked the Concepcion Volcano of Ometepe on horseback. I've seen with my own eyes the Crown of Thorns at Notre Dame in Paris as well as the Holy Grail at the Cathedral of Valencia in Spain. I've swam the beautiful beaches of the Philippines and climbed the Lanikai Pillboxes of Hawaii to catch the Golden Sunrise Sky. I have much more to see...
